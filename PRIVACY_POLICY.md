# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SpiceHut Admin ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the SpiceHut Admin application (the "App"). This App is intended for business users (restaurant owners, managers, and staff) who manage orders and operations.

If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the App.

## 1. Scope
This policy applies to the SpiceHut Admin mobile and desktop applications and any related services we provide. It does not cover third-party services linked from the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information**: email address, login credentials, and roles/permissions assigned by your organization.
- **Business details**: branch or location identifiers used to filter and manage orders.
- **Support communications**: messages and attachments you send to support.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device data**: device model, OS version, and app version.
- **Usage data**: screens viewed, actions taken, error logs, and performance metrics.
- **Network data**: IP address and connection type.

### 2.3 Order and Operational Data
- **Order data**: order number, items, totals, status, and timestamps.
- **Operational data**: printer discovery/connection details and branch selection.

### 2.4 Permissions and Sensors
The App may request permissions for features you use:
- **Camera**: to capture images for orders or management tasks.
- **Photo Library**: to select or save images.
- **Bluetooth**: to discover and connect to receipt printers.
- **Local Network**: to find printers on the same network.

You can deny permissions, but related features may not function.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Authenticate users and enforce role-based access.
- Display and manage orders for the correct branch.
- Provide in-app notifications and alert sounds while the App is in use.
- Support printing and device connectivity features.
- Monitor performance, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)
If you are in a jurisdiction that requires legal bases (e.g., GDPR), we process data based on:
- **Contractual necessity**: to provide the App and its features.
- **Legitimate interests**: to secure and improve the App.
- **Consent**: for optional permissions or marketing (if applicable).
- **Legal obligations**: to comply with law or requests by authorities.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:
- With **service providers** who help operate the App (e.g., hosting, database, analytics) under confidentiality obligations.
- With your **organization or branch** to enable administrative access and reporting.
- To comply with **legal requirements**, enforce policies, or protect rights and safety.

## 6. Data Retention
We retain data as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal or operational requirements. You may request deletion of account data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. International Transfers
Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We take steps to protect data during transfers as required by law.

## 9. Your Rights and Choices
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access or correct your data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Restrict or object to processing.
- Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity.

## 10. Notifications and Background Behavior
The App provides notifications and alert sounds while it is in use. If you want alerts while the App is not active, additional system-level notification services may be required. We will only enable those with your consent.

## 11. Children
The App is intended for business use and not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above and, where required, provide notice of material changes.
